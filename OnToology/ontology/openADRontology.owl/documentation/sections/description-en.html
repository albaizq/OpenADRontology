Figure 1 provides a general overview of the classes (only the top levels of the hierarchy) and the properties included in the OpenADR ontology. As it can be observed, there are some concepts reused from other well-known ontologies, such as those related to time entities.

Reports are an important entity in the OpenADR standard, since they support the exchange of information between VEN and VTNs. Figure 2 provides an overview of the classes and properties related to the OpenADR reports.
